How paths are calculated

The app derives group winners, runners-up and third-place candidates directly from your score inputs and the starting table. No forced scenario assumptions are applied.

Default scores are market-informed approximations, not betting advice. Third-place assignment is modelled by taking the highest-ranked available third-place team from each eligible pool; FIFA's exact allocation matrix can be more complex.

Bracket caveats

This is a scenario tool, not an official live bracket. It calculates group order using points, goal difference and goals scored. It does not model every disciplinary, fair-play or drawing-of-lots tiebreaker. Third-place allocation is approximate because FIFA's expanded format uses conditional assignments once the eight qualifying third-placed groups are known.
